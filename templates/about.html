Our Journey

From a simple idea to a comprehensive learning platform

2022 - The Beginning

Founded with a simple mission: make Python learning accessible to everyone. Started with basic lessons and a simple code editor.

2023 - Platform Growth

Launched interactive quizzes, project gallery, and advanced learning features. Reached 10,000 active learners milestone.

2024 - Innovation & Expansion

Introduced AI-powered learning paths, real-time collaboration features, and mobile learning applications. Serving 50,000+ students worldwide.

2025 - Future Vision

Expanding to other programming languages, introducing virtual reality learning experiences, and building a global community of developers.
